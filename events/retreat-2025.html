Retreat 2025 – Parma

16 October 2025 · CNR Institute of Neuroscience, Parma

The annual retreat gathers the entire AWARENET community for two days of scientific exchange, collaborative workshops, and networking opportunities.

Key highlights of the 2025 edition include:

  • Keynote lectures from leading neuroscientists on sensory integration and cognition.
  • Interactive poster sessions featuring the latest results from partner laboratories.
  • Career development roundtables dedicated to early-career researchers.

Registration opens on 1 September 2025. The full agenda and logistics pack will be circulated to all members via email.
